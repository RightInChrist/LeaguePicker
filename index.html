Instructions for League Director and Coaches

For the League Director:

  1. Setup:
    • Ensure that all coaches and players are added to the system.
  2. Evaluation Process:
    • Each coach evaluates every player by scoring them from 0 to 5 (including decimals).
  3. Data Entry:
    • After evaluations, enter player names and coach names into the system.
  4. Generating Team Assignments:
    • The system will calculate average scores and generate team assignments.

For Coaches:

  1. Evaluating Players:
    • Score each player from 0 to 5 (including decimals).
  2. Entering Data:
    • Ensure all evaluations are submitted before the deadline.
  3. Reviewing Team Assignments:
    • Review the suggested team assignments after scores are processed.
